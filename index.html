About The Author

Tia was born in Reading, but now resides in Dorset.

She was first published at the tender age of 9, and has continued to foster her passion for writing ever since. In 2020 it got personal when (after years of feeling alone in the struggle) she published a book sharing the sory of her life, living with scoliosis. Tia took this opportunity to connect to other people, and champion their stories aswell.

Tia is currently working on a new book, and a commissioned peice for local council. She hopes to continue to inspire growth of community with her work, and looks forward to attending some upcoming events.
