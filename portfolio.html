Recent Publications

Living with Scoliosis is a big challenge.

This is a collection of stories from sufferers of the condition; told in their own words, and included in this book with their permission, even though some contributors wished to remain anonymous.

You can read about their hopes and dreams, their pains and triumphs, all shared with a willingness to help and encourage others who find themselves in the same position.

The book also contains valuable information on the condition, treatments and other help available.

All proceeds from the sale of this book will be donated to the Scoliosis Association UK.
